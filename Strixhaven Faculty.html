Administration & Campus Services

Central Campus
Director Taiva
Head of School
Takes an active role in running the university to ensure balance between the colleges. Often found personally greeting new students.
Isabough
Head Librarian
A Treant who manages the Biblioplex. She oversees student workers and strictly controls entry to the restricted sections of the library.
Zoei
Student Life Administrator
A Satyr and Prismari alumna with boundless energy. Manages student housing, dormitories, and organizes campus-wide events like balls and game nights.
Professor Orvlo
General Studies
A Vedalken who teaches magical composition. Serves as a volunteer librarian specializing in dispelling enchantments students place on books.
Gyome
Head Chef
A Troll master chef. While not a professor, he teaches a General Studies course on magical cooking. His ingredient choices can be... unusual.
Melvi Le Donto
Lab Administrator
Leads the student workers responsible for cleaning, organizing, and maintaining research labs across all six campuses.
"Coach" Shtomp
Mage Tower Coach
An Orc alumnus who struggled with academics but dominated Mage Tower. Now manages the Stadium and intramural fields.
Dramitia
Arts Society Manager
A Tiefling Silverquill alumna who manages the Performing Arts Society and directs the annual school play.
"Cleaner"
Grounds Automaton
Created by Professor Losheel. An automaton that obsessively cleans and repairs the campus grounds and supervises student workers.
Yedora
Head Groundskeeper
A Treant who maintains the campus flora and oversees student groundskeeping jobs.
